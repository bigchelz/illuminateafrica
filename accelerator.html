About Our Accelerator Service

Upanuzi is more than an accelerator; it's a transformative service designed to empower established businesses aiming to scale and accelerate their growth.

We offer a comprehensive ecosystem that equips your business with the tools and support needed to achieve significant progress in a short timeframe.

What We Offer:

Transformational Journey: At Upanuzi, your business embarks on a transformational journey. We cater to businesses at different growth stages, from well-established to those in early phases. Our primary objective is to help your business reach a dramatically improved state within a three-month timeframe.

Intense Support: Upanuzi offers an environment of unmatched intensity where it's all about your business, all the time. Our team, fellow founders, alumni, speakers, and investors are dedicated to supporting your success. This environment fosters exceptional motivation, a crucial factor for tackling the challenges of scaling a business.

Investment Support: Upanuzi invests $100,000 in your business through a unique structure: $100,000 in exchange for either debt or equity based on the terms sheet and nature of the particular venture.

Customized Guidance: We structure businesses into focused groups led by dedicated advisors. These groups offer one-on-one and group guidance within an intimate setting.

Ubuntu: Ubuntu is your digital connection to fellow founders. It's a blend of social networking, knowledge-sharing, and professional networking. Here, you can showcase your expertise, seek advice, and tap into the extensive knowledge base within the Upanuzi community.

Events and Networking: Our service includes various events and networking opportunities :

  • Scaling Retreat: Kick off your journey with a three-day, in-person retreat.

  • Enlightening Talks: We invite prominent figures from the business world to share their insights and insider stories.

  • >Market Entry and Customer Acquisition: We help you launch your products effectively and prepare for community site launches.

  • Regular Meetups: Join us for meetups featuring special guests and opportunities to connect.

  • Upanuzi Pitch: JAt the Upanuzi Pitch, businesses present their achievements to a select audience of investors and media representatives, creating unparalleled opportunities for investment and media coverage.

  • Post-Acceleration Support: Our support extends beyond the three-month program, offering:

  • Ongoing Advice: Year-round office hours provide continuous support and guidance.

  • Series A Program: Access one-to-one support, pitch practice, fundraising guides, templates, and workshops to excel in your Series A round.

  • Specialized Founder Communities: Access tailored communities to connect with like-minded founders.

  • Alumni Preview Day: Gain early access to new startups for collaboration and investment.

  • Credibility and Exposure: Leverage the Upanuzi name to attract investors and media attention.

  • Startup Directory: Showcase your business in the Upanuzi Startup Directory for easy discovery.
